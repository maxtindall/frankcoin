How it works

The program on Solana holds its own mint authority. No person, wallet or company can issue a FRANK by signing for it — the only way a FRANK comes into existence is that someone submitted work the program checked and accepted.

Each miner carries a challenge of their own. Finding a nonce that hashes with it to a digest of sufficiently many leading zeros earns the reward, and the challenge immediately rolls forward. The proof you just spent is worthless a second later, and there is no list of used proofs to maintain — against a new challenge, an old nonce is simply a bad guess.

Supply
1,000,000,000 FRANKS, nine decimals. The cap is enforced on-chain and cannot be exceeded by anyone, including its author.
Issuance
Mined from zero. No pre-mine, no founder allocation, no admin mint instruction anywhere in the program.
Reward
500 FRANKS per accepted proof at genesis, halving with each tranche of supply — 500M, then 250M, then 125M, and so on.
Proof
keccak(challenge ‖ miner ‖ nonce) must carry at least difficulty leading zero bits.
Custody
This site holds no keys and takes no cut. Every transaction is signed by your own wallet, and you can mine without this page at all.
Source
The program, its tests and this miner are public. Anyone may read them, fork them, or write a faster miner.
